Core Pathway is an offender focused platform, created to accelerate positive change in the justice system and improve outcomes.

In prison

Supporting prisoners on using cell time productively, Staff managing their daily activities and Family to stay connected.

Through the gate

Preparing prisoners for successful re-entry into society.

In the community

Helping prisoners navigate into their everyday life and dutie

Core Pathway is a collection of flexible, personalised apps and programs that can be configured to meet the needs of the individual

Organisation skills

We can provide planning and organisational tools to support active re-entry.

Communication

Pathway enables offenders to send and receive on average 18million messages per year for current users/customers.

Education

Pathway provides on average 50,000 ebooks to offenders per year—offender’s access to ebooks on tablets to read at their own pace.

Health

Register for health services, health visits and consultations via video communications.

Wellbeing

Media, radio, podcasts, websites, e-games Pathway delivered on average 2,000 songs downloaded onto tablet devices per year.
